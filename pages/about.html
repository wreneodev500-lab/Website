Our Story

About Diproxy

A one‑person studio building sci‑fi worlds with impossible ambition.

Mission

Space is not
the final frontier —
imagination is.

Diproxy began in 2021 as a personal experiment: could one developer create sci‑fi games with the same sense of scale, atmosphere, and mystery as the classics? What started as a hobby quickly became a full studio identity.

Based in Palm Bay, Florida, Diproxy is a solo operation — design, code, art, music, and writing all crafted by one developer with a stubborn love for world‑building. Every system, every UI element, every voxel is placed with intention.

  • [01] Player agency above all else
  • [02] Atmosphere as a first-class feature
  • [03] Small teams can build big worlds
  • [04] Ship it when it feels right
History

The Journey

2021

Diproxy Founded

The studio begins as a solo project. First prototypes built in a tiny home office in Palm Bay.

2022

First Public Release

Early experiments evolve into full projects. The first players discover Diproxy’s signature sci‑fi style.

2023

World‑Building Expands

Development shifts toward larger, more systemic games — voxel engines, survival mechanics, and atmospheric storytelling.

2024

Terrasasters

The studio’s biggest milestone. A fully‑voxel survival world built from scratch by one developer.

2025

New Projects Begin

Multiple prototypes evolve into full titles — including Bombling Sweeper and Structural Integrity INC.
